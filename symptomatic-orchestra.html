‹ Symptomatic Orchestra

Symptomatic Orchestra is an interactive artistic installation. Visitors are invited to become part of a graphic and musical ecosystem, and thus interact with it, observe it, listen to it... In this digital and living hybrid environment, the visitor can choose to create a emotionally loaded virus having its own personality, and belonging to one of the three families which make up the ecosystem. This project has been realised for the Multimedia degree, at Gobelins Paris school.

TEAM : Alexia Dupré-Doàn (design) – Julie Flogeac (design) – Christophe Massolin (development) – Bertrand Souriau (development)
