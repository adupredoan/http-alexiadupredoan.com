‹ Pabloscope

We had the chance to meet the Muséee Picasso Paris with Gobelins, and they gave us the brief for their new mobile application. As the museum is reopening in fall 2014, they needed a way to communicate about the national event, not only inside the museum, but an mobile application that can be experienced everywhere, every time. We worked during 3 month to find a idea, design it and produce it. Our though was : Discover the Pablo Picasso Universe. The user can browse trough constellations, the immensity of his work. That leaded to the black and white layout with the dots.

Team : Alexia Dupré-Doàn (Design) – Louis Ansa (Design) – Christophe Massolin (Development) – Kolin Weidmann (Development)
